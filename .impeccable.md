# Sea Robin Classic — Design Context

## Users
The 24 invite-only anglers and the M.O.C. of an annual Mid-Atlantic surf fishing
tournament (men, 21+). Primary context: **on the beach, on a phone, one-handed,
in bright sun or at night** — a PWA. Secondary context: the M.O.C. reviewing
every scorecard at end-of-tournament. Jobs: log a catch fast, watch the live
pulse, ask the rulebook, review/score cards, browse records & career badges.

## Brand Personality
Ceremonial, tongue-in-cheek, exclusive-club. "The M.O.C.'s word is final."
Rugged coastal with reverent gravitas and a wink (Grand Robin, The Coveted Sea
Robin, the Derek Clause). Three words: **ceremonial, rugged, wry.**

## Aesthetic Direction
Dark-default (night fishing / sun glare), clean and contemporary. Display =
Bricolage Grotesque, body/UI = Hanken Grotesk. Single ocean-teal accent
(`--flare`) with **gold reserved for honors** (records, Champion, Grand Robin,
Full Monty). SVG line icons only, no emoji in chrome. Full light + dark theming
via CSS variables on `[data-theme]`. Motion follows Emil Kowalski principles
(custom easing, press feedback, staggered entrances, `prefers-reduced-motion`).

## Design Principles
1. **Legible in sun and dark** — strong contrast, generous type, no glassy low-contrast surfaces.
2. **Touch-first** — 44px minimum targets; thumb-reachable actions; bottom nav.
3. **Ceremony with a wink** — honors feel earned (gold, badges); copy has personality but never at the cost of clarity.
4. **Token discipline** — colors, spacing, motion from the shared system; the accent is rare on purpose (60/30/10).
5. **Offline-first calm** — the UI never blocks on the network; states are always resolved (loading / empty / error).
